Other Stuff of mine

This is documentation of Columbia Scuba's October 2005 trip to Cozumel that was interrupted by Hurricane Wilma. The schedule was for October 15-22, 2005. The trip was interruped by the hurricane and we ended up staying a little longer than expected.

Our stay was at Scuba Club Cozumel. This is the same place Columbia Scuba has been going for several years. They treated us wonderfully throughout the whole ordeal.

If you are interested in reading about what we went through see the blow by blow recap.
